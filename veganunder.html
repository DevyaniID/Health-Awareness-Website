Underweight Vegan Weekly Diet Plan

Breakfast: Almond Milk Smoothie + Peanut Butter Toast
Calories: ~500 kcal
Tip: Blend banana, oats, almond milk, and peanut butter.
Lunch: Chickpea Salad + Quinoa + Avocado
Calories: ~600 kcal
Tip: Rich in protein and healthy fats.
Snack: Trail Mix + Coconut Water
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: A good source of iron and magnesium.
Dinner: Vegan Dal + Brown Rice + Stir-fried Veggies
Calories: ~550 kcal
Tip: Use ghee substitute like olive oil.
Breakfast: Soya Milk + Banana + Chia Pudding
Calories: ~480 kcal
Tip: Chia seeds are high in omega-3s.
Lunch: Lentil Curry + Millet + Cucumber Salad
Calories: ~620 kcal
Tip: Add lemon juice for better iron absorption.
Snack: Banana + Roasted Seeds
Calories: ~270 kcal
Tip: Sunflower and pumpkin seeds boost energy.
Dinner: Vegan Pasta + Mixed Veggies + Nut Sauce
Calories: ~600 kcal
Tip: Use cashew or almond sauce for creaminess.
Breakfast: Vegan Pancakes with Maple Syrup & Fruits
Calories: ~520 kcal
Tip: Use oat flour and almond milk.
Lunch: Rajma + Brown Rice + Steamed Broccoli
Calories: ~640 kcal
Tip: Use olive oil for sautéing.
Snack: Dates + Almonds + Soy Yogurt
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Great source of calcium and iron.
Dinner: Tofu Stir-fry with Quinoa & Veggies
Calories: ~580 kcal
Tip: Use sesame oil for extra flavor.
Breakfast: Avocado Toast + Orange Juice
Calories: ~500 kcal
Tip: Add hemp seeds for extra protein.
Lunch: Vegan Biryani + Cucumber Raita
Calories: ~630 kcal
Tip: Use coconut yogurt as raita base.
Snack: Peanut Chikki + Herbal Tea
Calories: ~290 kcal
Tip: Natural sugar boost for energy.
Dinner: Lentil Soup + Multigrain Bread + Sautéed Veggies
Calories: ~570 kcal
Tip: Add parsley and garlic for digestion.
Breakfast: Vegan Upma + Coconut Chutney
Calories: ~510 kcal
Tip: Add peas and carrots for extra nutrients.
Lunch: Chole + Millet Roti + Salad
Calories: ~650 kcal
Tip: Use olive oil for cooking.
Snack: Fruit Smoothie Bowl + Nuts
Calories: ~320 kcal
Tip: Top with flaxseeds and granola.
Dinner: Vegetable Tofu Curry + Jeera Rice
Calories: ~600 kcal
Tip: Add coconut milk for creamy taste.
Breakfast: Vegan Dosa + Sambar
Calories: ~540 kcal
Tip: Use fermented batter for probiotics.
Lunch: Vegan Kofta Curry + Steamed Rice
Calories: ~660 kcal
Tip: Use soya chunks in kofta.
Snack: Makhana + Vegan Lassi
Calories: ~280 kcal
Tip: Roast makhana in olive oil.
Dinner: Sprout Salad + Grilled Sweet Potato
Calories: ~570 kcal
Tip: Add lemon juice and pepper.
Breakfast: Vegan French Toast + Dates Shake
Calories: ~550 kcal
Tip: Use oat milk and flax eggs.
Lunch: Vegetable Pulav + Cashew Curry
Calories: ~670 kcal
Tip: Add raisins for sweetness.
Snack: Dry Fruits + Oat Bar
Calories: ~310 kcal

Calories: ~600 kcal
Tip: Use plant-based butter or oils.
