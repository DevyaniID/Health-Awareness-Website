Vegetarian Weekly Diet Plan (Normal Weight)

Breakfast: Vegetable Upma + Coconut Chutney
Calories: ~350 kcal
Tip: Use minimal oil and add mixed vegetables for fiber.
Lunch: Rajma + Brown Rice + Cucumber Salad
Calories: ~550 kcal
Tip: Soak rajma overnight and cook without cream for lightness.
Snack: Roasted Makhana + Green Tea
Calories: ~150 kcal
Tip: Dry roast with a pinch of pink salt.
Dinner: Palak Paneer + Chapati + Buttermilk
Calories: ~450 kcal
Tip: Use low-fat paneer and drink buttermilk to aid digestion.
Breakfast: Moong Dal Chilla + Mint Chutney
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Add grated veggies and avoid over-oiling.
Lunch: Mixed Vegetable Curry + Multigrain Roti + Curd
Calories: ~550 kcal
Tip: Include seasonal vegetables for best nutrition.
Snack: Fruit Salad + Lemon Water
Calories: ~180 kcal
Tip: Avoid added sugar, use fresh fruits only.
Dinner: Tofu Stir Fry + Brown Rice
Calories: ~450 kcal
Tip: Light soy sauce and sesame oil add flavor without calories.
Breakfast: Poha with Peanuts + Chai
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Add lemon juice and coriander for extra flavor and vitamin C.
Lunch: Vegetable Pulao + Raita + Salad
Calories: ~500 kcal
Tip: Use less oil and add peas, beans, and carrots for fiber.
Snack: Coconut Water + Handful of Walnuts
Calories: ~180 kcal
Tip: A refreshing, low-calorie electrolyte boost.
Dinner: Vegetable Khichdi + Buttermilk
Calories: ~420 kcal
Tip: Light and easy to digest. Perfect for mid-week digestion support.
Breakfast: Idli + Sambar + Coconut Chutney
Calories: ~350 kcal
Tip: Fermented foods help with digestion and gut health.
Lunch: Chole + Jeera Rice + Onion Salad
Calories: ~550 kcal
Tip: Use boiled chana and mild spices to keep it light.
Snack: Sprout Chaat + Herbal Tea
Calories: ~200 kcal
Tip: Add lemon, tomato, onion and chaat masala for taste.
Dinner: Stuffed Capsicum + Chapati
Calories: ~430 kcal
Tip: Bake the capsicum with low-fat paneer and veggies.
Breakfast: Dhokla + Mint Chutney
Calories: ~280 kcal
Tip: Steam it fresh in the morning for a light start.
Lunch: Bhindi Masala + Bajra Roti + Curd
Calories: ~500 kcal
Tip: Cook bhindi with minimal oil and whole spices.
Snack: Banana + Almonds
Calories: ~200 kcal
Tip: A great energy booster pre-evening walk.
Dinner: Matar Paneer + Roti + Buttermilk
Calories: ~460 kcal
Tip: Use less cream and fresh peas for protein and taste.
Breakfast: Rava Dosa + Coconut Chutney
Calories: ~350 kcal
Tip: Add grated carrot or beet for nutrition.
Lunch: Kadhi + Rice + Beans Stir Fry

Dinner: Baingan Bharta + Roti + Salad

Breakfast: Aloo Paratha + Curd
Calories: ~400 kcal
Tip: Use minimal ghee and serve with plain low-fat curd.
Lunch: Veg Biryani + Boondi Raita + Onion Salad
