Vegetarian Weekly Diet Plan – For Overweight Individuals

Breakfast: Moong Dal Chilla + Mint Chutney
Calories: ~250 kcal
Tip: Add veggies like spinach or grated carrot for fiber.
Lunch: Quinoa Pulao + Mixed Veg Sabzi + Curd
Calories: ~450 kcal
Tip: Quinoa is high-protein and helps manage hunger.
Snack: Sprouts Salad + Lemon Water
Calories: ~150 kcal
Tip: Rich in fiber and supports metabolism.
Dinner: Lauki Soup + Grilled Paneer Cubes + Salad
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Light dinner promotes better digestion.
Breakfast: Vegetable Upma + Green Tea
Calories: ~270 kcal
Tip: Add more vegetables for extra fiber.
Lunch: Brown Rice + Rajma + Cucumber Raita
Calories: ~480 kcal
Tip: Use less oil; rajma provides plant-based protein.
Snack: Roasted Makhana + Herbal Tea
Calories: ~160 kcal
Tip: Great low-calorie snack for weight control.
Dinner: Tofu Stir Fry + Broccoli Soup
Calories: ~310 kcal
Tip: Light, protein-rich dinner.
Breakfast: Besan Cheela + Tomato Chutney
Calories: ~260 kcal
Tip: Cook on non-stick tawa with minimal oil.
Lunch: Mixed Veg Curry + Bajra Roti + Buttermilk
Calories: ~450 kcal
Tip: Bajra is high in fiber and good for weight loss.
Snack: Cucumber + Carrot Sticks with Hummus
Calories: ~170 kcal
Tip: Great low-calorie, protein-rich snack.
Dinner: Palak Soup + Grilled Mushrooms
Calories: ~290 kcal
Tip: Avoid butter or cream in soup.
Breakfast: Oats Idli + Coconut Chutney
Calories: ~280 kcal
Tip: Steam instead of fry; oats keep you full.
Lunch: Vegetable Khichdi + Low-fat Curd
Calories: ~460 kcal
Tip: Use brown rice and moong dal for digestion.
Snack: Buttermilk + 1 Banana
Calories: ~150 kcal
Tip: Hydrating and keeps sugar levels balanced.
Dinner: Cabbage Soup + Mixed Veg Cutlet (baked)
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Add garlic and herbs for metabolism boost.
Breakfast: Ragi Dosa + Tomato Chutney
Calories: ~250 kcal
Tip: Ragi helps in managing blood sugar and weight.
Lunch: Chapati + Bottle Gourd Curry + Salad
Calories: ~440 kcal
Tip: Lauki is excellent for reducing belly fat.
Snack: Apple Slices + Almonds (5)
Calories: ~170 kcal
Tip: Almonds provide good fats in small quantities.
Dinner: Tomato Soup + Steamed Veggies
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Skip croutons to keep calories low.
Breakfast: Idli + Sambar
Calories: ~270 kcal
Tip: Limit coconut in chutney, sambar adds protein.
Lunch: Whole Wheat Roti + Bhindi Fry (Low Oil) + Buttermilk
Calories: ~450 kcal
Tip: Bhindi is low-carb and great for weight loss.
Snack: Herbal Tea + Roasted Chana
Calories: ~160 kcal
Tip: Protein-packed and crunchy snack.
Dinner: Methi Soup + Sautéed Tofu
Calories: ~290 kcal
Tip: Helps reduce insulin resistance.
Breakfast: Vegetable Dalia + Green Tea
Calories: ~260 kcal
Tip: High in fiber and keeps you full longer.
Lunch: Masoor Dal + Brown Rice + Mixed Veg
Calories: ~460 kcal
Tip: Red lentils digest easily and are low-fat.
Snack: Fruit Bowl + Lemon Water
Calories: ~150 kcal
Tip: Use low-sugar fruits like papaya, apple.
Dinner: Zucchini Soup + Moong Salad
Calories: ~300 kcal
Tip: Light yet protein-rich to end the week well.
